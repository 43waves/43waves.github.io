Mute Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

Overview

Mute ("the App") is developed by 43waves. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information - We don't collect names, emails, or account data
  • No usage data sent to our servers - Your preferences stay on your device
  • No tracking by us - We don't track your behavior or location
  • No analytics - We don't use any analytics or tracking services

What Stays On Your Device

  • Theme preference - Your selected theme (System/Light/Dark)
  • Silent duration - Your configured auto-reset duration
  • Reset time - Scheduled time for ringer mode reset
  • Ad cooldown - Timestamp to limit ad frequency

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements powered by Google AdMob. When ads are shown, Google may collect certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • Ad interaction data

You can learn more about Google's advertising practices and opt-out options at Google's Ads Settings.

Ad Frequency

  • Ads are only shown on app launch after initial setup is complete
  • A cooldown period ensures you won't see ads too frequently

Permissions

Do Not Disturb Access

Required to change your device's ringer mode between Normal, Vibrate, and Silent. On Android 6.0+, setting the ringer mode to Silent also enables Do Not Disturb - this is how Android works by design. This permission is used solely for the app's core functionality.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The advertising shown is handled by Google AdMob, which has its own policies regarding personalized advertising.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at 43waves@gmail.com.
